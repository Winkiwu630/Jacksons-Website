Recycling

Recycling isn't just the subject of my first published book — it's something I care about deeply. As an Environmental Specialist for four Twin Cities suburbs, sustainability is part of my day job as much as it is my creative work.

Recycla-Bull grew out of that passion: a desire to introduce young readers to the idea that the choices we make every day — sorting our recycling, reducing waste, taking care of the land around us — really do matter. If a picture book about a heroic steer can plant that seed in even one kid, it's worth it.

About the Book

Recycla-Bull

Children's Picture Book

When the animals on Farmer Jo's farm wake to find their home covered in litter, they call on RECYCLA-BULL to help clean it up. The heroic steer teaches the animals and young readers how to recycle right, reduce waste, and make sustainable choices to protect their natural resources.
