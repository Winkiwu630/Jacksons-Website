Geomorphology

Somewhere after author, astronaut, and professional athlete, Fluvial Geomorphology Professor landed on my list of dream jobs. During the COVID lockdown in Minnesota, I put that passion to work and created a series of educational videos on geomorphology — the study of mountains, hills, rivers, and the forces that shape them. Rivers in particular hold a special fascination for me.

Below are the locations featured in the video series:

  • Minnewashta Regional Park
  • Interstate Park (MN & WI)
  • Devil's Kettle (North Shore)
  • Badlands National Park
Most of this information is from my memory of lectures years ago. Real geomorphologists are welcome to correct me if I'm wrong about anything.
